Shiv Patel is a Photographer based in the Delaware county area.

shivpatelshots@gmail.com

 

Born and raised in Johannesburg, South Africa, Shiv came to the United States in 2014. Currently a college Junior attending Penn State University, majoring in Computer Science.

He got his start assisting his local Hindu Temple on photo shoots for their own cultural festivals. That quickly led to commissions from his friends and family before he graduated from high school he was already steadily in demand in Philadelphia’s vibrant creative community.
